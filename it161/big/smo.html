Social Media Optimization

Social media optimization (SMO) aims to increase online presence by using social media networks. Most companies want to capture more followers on social media in order to increase brand awareness and market products as it is a low-cost way (Will Kenton).

SMO Importance

SMO is important for companies because it results in the following benefits (Dominique Jackson): It strengthens their brand. It helps to get more visibility. It connects companies to their audience. It generates leads.

SMO Best Practices

There are several best practices that result in better SEO as follows (Best Practices For Social Media Optimization):

  • Creating your strategy: a well-defined strategy can help a company to achieve the best results from SMO.
  • Doing keyword search: we need to know what interests our audience. Then extract the related keywords and use them in our content in social networks in order to increase the chance of content visibility.
  • Optimizing your profile: generating a good profile including username, profile photos, bio is a crucial step towards good SMO.
  • Improving content: having high-quality content can be appealing to both users and search engines. Search engines rank your content higher than others and users tend to be attracted more.
  • Using multimedia content: in addition to text, we need to add images and videos which can increase visibility.
  • Using hashtags: hashtags are one of the most important features in social networks that users use to find their favorite content.
  • Shortening the length of your posts: some social networks such as Twitter impose some limitations in terms of the length of tweets. Therefore, we need to consider the length of posts using these social networks.
  • Encouraging interactions: we can increase interactions by asking questions and leaving comments.
  • Following a schedule: it is important to post your messages in the best time that can be seen by your audiences.
